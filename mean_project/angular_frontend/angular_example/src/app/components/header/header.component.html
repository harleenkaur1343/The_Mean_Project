Welcome!

Embracing India's rich heritage, Noormahal Palace endorses the opulent royalty of the era of Indian maharajas, flaunting an enchanting fusion of elements inspired from traditional Mughal and Rajputana schools of architecture. Noormahal Palace Hotel in Karnal exudes warmth and comfort for all its guests by preserving the legacy of India's deep-rooted past heritage. Stunningly set in vast expanse of natural splendour, Noormahal Palace is truly a one of its kind Palace in the region; an epitome of grandiose.
